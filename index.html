Step Out of Your Comfort Zone!

Comfort zone: the boundaries of your current experience and capabilities where you operate on autopilot, relying on established routines and habits.

Growth zone: state of constructive discomfort where you transform potential into reality.

Unrealized potential: impossible tasks that you could achieve if you overcome the obstacles preventing you from doing so.

Let's view an example to see how our comfort zone grows or shrinks over time. Scroll down to the end and watch how choosing to do things outside your comfort zone affect you.

But the opposite is also true! Scroll back up to see your comfort zone shrink if you decided not to try these new things.

If you don't conciously try new things outside of your comfort zone, your world shrinks, and you limit your potential.
